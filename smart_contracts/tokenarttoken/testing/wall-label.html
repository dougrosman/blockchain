Token Art Tokens (TARTs) are unique works of digital art, purchasable on the Ethereum blockchain. Each TART is simultaneously a work of art, as well as a tool to create works of art. With nearly 90 quadrillion unique TARTs available to own, their artistic potential is virtually limitless.

Token Art Token explores ideas of art and aesthetic value, and how digital ownership through blockchain might accelerate art's hyper-commodification.
