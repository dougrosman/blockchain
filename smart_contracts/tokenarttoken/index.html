Token Art Token

Token Art Tokens (TARTs) are simultaneously art objects and instructions for creating new art objects. TARTs are comprised of two pieces of information: a grid size, and a color palette. These properties are chosen at random at the moment of generation. Anyone who generates a TART is guaranteed a unique pairing of grid size and color palette. When you own a TART, you also own each pattern made possible by your TART.

TARTs will soon be available for purchase and exchange on the Ethereum blockchain. To be notified about their availability, please subscribe using the form below.

Though they are not yet available to own, you can experiment with generating TARTs.

Press the N key to generate a random TART. If you've found a TART to you like, you can press the G key to generate new art works with your TART.
